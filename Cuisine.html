Cuisines of Kerala

Kerala's cuisine is a symphony of flavors and aromas that will delight your taste buds and transport you to a world of culinary excellence. Enriched by its geographical diversity, the state's cuisine offers a range of culinary experiences that cater to every palate.

Traditional Kerala Sadya

The Kerala Sadya is a lavish traditional feast served on a banana leaf. It features an array of vegetarian dishes, from aromatic curries to crispy appams and sweet payasam, showcasing the essence of Kerala's culinary heritage.

Seafood Extravaganza

With its extensive coastline, Kerala is a seafood lover's paradise. Taste the freshness of coastal catches, from spicy fish curries to succulent prawn dishes, and savor the true essence of Kerala's coastal cuisine.

Malabar Biryani Delight

Malabar biryani is a fragrant rice dish cooked with aromatic spices and succulent pieces of chicken or mutton. It's a culinary masterpiece that reflects the rich heritage of Kerala's Malabar region.

Irresistible Kerala Snacks

Kerala's snacks are perfect for tea-time indulgence. Try banana chips, crispy tapioca, and the famous Kerala parippu vada for a delightful combination of flavors and textures.
